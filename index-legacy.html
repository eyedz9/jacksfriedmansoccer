SCOUTING REPORT

"Friedman will be a huge riser In a soon-to-come rankings update and could be a breakout star in 2024/2025. He is one of the, if not the most, athletic keepers in California from the class of 2027. His quick-twitch response is second to nobody I have seen, as he reacts incredibly fast, allowing him to make saves when an attacker is in on goal alone. He can cover his near post, knowing he has the athleticism and ability to get to a ball at the far post. He is unafraid to come out and challenge, understanding when to sweep the ball or when to get big and take away an angle. The way he understands positioning and angles is next level, and he is completely switched on as he moves quickly with the flow of play. He is a fiery leader and a vocal keeper. Friedman was an All-Conference ECNL Selection last season" - Prep Soccer.
